Rules

MISSION STATEMENT
  • To provide a safe, clean, sober living environment for recovery from drug and alcohol addiction
  • Our goal is to help you cultivate the skills needed to maintain a healthy sober lifestyle through an Active Recovery Process focused on responsibility, community, and service.
ACTIVE RECOVERY

Clients must by actively involved in their own recovery including:

  1. Meeting Attendance - mandatory house meetings and outside meetings
  2. Working with a Sponsor - mandatory sponsor within the first 30 days or restriction will occur
  3. Working the 12 Steps - checking in weekly with progress through the steps
  4. Being of Service - through meeting commitments, helping house brothers, etc.
ZERO TOLERANCE POLICY

Clients must be involved in an Active Recovery Process. Abstinence from all mind-altering chemicals is mandatory. All clients will strive for a zero tolerance against drug and/or alcohol usage. Anyone caught using drugs, alcohol, or any other form of mind altering substances will be excited immediately from the Stepping Stones House Program. Please read the Drug Testing Policy posted in the house. Stealing of food, personal items, etc. or use of another person's items without permission will also result in expulsion from the program.

RELIGION- This is a recovery home, not a religious program. Please consider differences in spiritual beliefs and practices. Do not make assumptions or force your beliefs upon others.

GANG- No gang activity or gang related behavior (including racial slurs, neighborhood graffiti, etc. This is a recovery home, not a prison yard! No weapons of any kind-NO EXCEPTIONS. Any violations will lead to expulsion.

Parole/Probation- Agent contact does occur. "When asked" a progress report will be given.

Random Urine Analysis Drug Testing- Any refusal or positive test is an automatic exit from the program. No exceptions-you use, you lose.

FIRST 30 DAYS

Clients with less than 30 days in the Stepping Stones Program must check-in at 6:30 p.m. Monday through Friday. Residents may not leave after 6:30 p.m. check-in without approval from the House Manager. Clients are to be working, looking for work, going to school, attending meetings etc. during this time.

Saturdays- Must be in house unless checked out by Sponsor or House Buddy.

Sundays- Must be in house unless checked out by Sponsor or House Buddy or on a "Family Pass." Attend our Mandatory Sunday House Meeting and Dinner at 6:00 p.m.

No sponsor within the first 30 days

House Restriction will remain in effect until sponsorship is acquired. This includes: Extra chores, NO weekend pass, NO family visits. If 45 days pass and you still have no sponsor, you will be considered a non-serious member of recovery and asked to exit the program.

OVER 30 DAYS

Clients with over 30 days in the program have the following curfew: Sunday through Thursdays 11:00 p.m. Friday and Saturday 12:00 (midnight). Clients are still required to attend House Meeting and at least three outside meetings weekly.

MEDICATIONS

Any prescribed medication must be listed on your intake paperwork and turned into Administrative Staff during your intake for approval. Medication will be kept locked up and dispensed only by staff. It is the client's responsibility to get any needed refills on his medications. The following medications are NOT permitted at Stepping Stones: Opiates, Codeine, Vicodin, Morphine, Methodone, Valium, Barbituates, Benzodiazepines, nor any other habit-forming medications. There are also many "over-the counter-medications" that will cause a "dirty" drug test.

MEETING ATTENDANCE

Participation in the Recovery Program of Alcoholics Anonymous (AA) or Narcotics Anonymous (NA) is essential. Verification of AA/NA attendance is mandatory for the first 30 days and can be obtained by placing your meeting slip in the 7th tradition basket as it is being passed around in every meeting. After the first 30 days clients are on the "honor system." Forging of meeting cards will result in loss of privileges and extension of 30-day restrictions.

Clients must attend meetings during the day unless they are working, in which case them may attend an AA or NA meeting in the evening after 6:30 p.m. Clients who are "restricted" to the house for the weekend for not adhering to House Rules can still work. However, clients must be back for 6:30 p.m. check-in.

MANDATORY MEETINGS

There are 2 mandatory House Meetings that all clients and staff must attend.

  1. Tuesdays at 7:30p.m.- Step Study/Book Study
  2. Sundays at 7:30p.m.-House Issues and Recovery Check-In
DAILY CHECK-IN/CHORE

Daily Check-in at 6:30 unless you are working. You must call to notify the house manager if your will be late or you will receive extra chores. If tardiness continues, you will be exited from the program. Clients with over 30 days do not have a 6:30 check-in on Saturdays and may leave Saturday morning to return no later than 11:55 for midnight curfew. Chores assigned by staff will also be done at 6:30. Every other Sunday, "thorough cleaning" chores are done at 6p.m.

LIGHTS

All lights must be turned off after leaving an area. Extra chores will be assigned to anyone who does not follow this rule.

HOUSE OFF-LIMITS

The house is off limits Monday-Friday from 10:00 a.m.-3:00 p.m. Clients should be working, looking for work, going to school, or attending meetings. Clients are permitted to come home for days off work. NO TV AND NO VISITORS between 10:00 a.m. and 3:00 p.m.

VISITING POLICY

ALL visitors must be actively clean and sober. Visitors are welcome in the living/dining room areas and back yard only. They are not allowed in bedrooms or the TV room. TV room is for residents only

Weekdays- Only Recovery related visitors permitted (Sponsor, NA/AA participants etc.)

Weekends-Family and friends may visit, but please be considerate of other residents and limit visit to two hours.

NO OVERNIGHT GUESTS-PERIOD -Anyone caught sneaking anyone in/out will be exited from the program.

MEETING ATTENDANCE SHEET

It is the responsibility of each client to use the meeting attendance sheet. Failure to do so can result in a "House Restriction" (HR) for the following weekend. If late for any of the required check-ins, please call.

SPONSOR PASS

Sponsor passes are available from the House Manager for clients in their first 30 days who want to meet with their sponsor away from Stepping Stones. The sponsor must be introduced to staff initially. Passes must be filled out completely and correctly and turned in ahead of time for approval. A pass does not exclude a client from check-in time, mandatory meetings, or chores.

SMOKING

SMOKING IN THE HOUSE WILL RESULT IN IMMEDIATE EXPULSION

In addition to daily chores, there is a Sunday afternoon cleaning time from 6-7 p.m. "Super Scrub" is a more detailed cleaning than regular chores. Follow your house manager's direction for exact instructions about the task you will be doing. Failure to attend Super Scrub can result in expulsion.

Failure to clean dishes or kitchen after use will result in loss of privileges. NO FOOD allowed in bedrooms or living room. Personal food items left out will be disposed of. Refrigerator must be maintained at all times (dispose of old food). Do not eat another person's food. Everyone has a designated area.
